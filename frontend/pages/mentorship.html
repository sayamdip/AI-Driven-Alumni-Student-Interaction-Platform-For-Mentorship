Mentorship Program

Our mentorship program bridges the gap between alumni and students. Experienced alumni offer guidance, career advice, and industry insights to help students achieve their goals.

Benefits:

  • Get career advice from industry professionals
  • Expand your professional network
  • Receive guidance on projects, internships, and jobs

How it Works:

  1. Sign up as a student or alumni
  2. Match based on department and interest
  3. Start one-on-one mentorship sessions
